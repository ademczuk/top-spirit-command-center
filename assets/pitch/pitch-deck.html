Executive pitch deck — Top Spirit × Angel Software Solutions

Steering-committee preview. Speaker-ready slide narrative. Pair with the rendered storyboard in assets/mockups/.

Slide 1 — Title

Top Spirit (Marussia) × Angel Software Solutions

Create value now — while ERP and BI decisions are still open

  • Process review + automation + AI literacy
  • Built on file exports inside Microsoft 365 E3
  • Human in control of every financial and customer-facing decision

Speaker note: Set the frame — this is not an ERP project, it is a value-now, capability-stays programme.

Slide 2 — The situation

  • ERP migration and BI-platform choice are pending.
  • Teams are already stretched: manual order entry, fragmented reports, payment reconciliation by hand.
  • Waiting 12–18 months for a new ERP means leaving savings and capability on the table.

Speaker note: Validate with the sponsor — "Does this match what you are seeing?"

Slide 3 — The promise

Deliver measurable savings and capability now, using Microsoft 365 E3 and file exports only.

  • No new licences without an evidence-backed business case.
  • ERP-agnostic: everything survives the migration.
  • Capability stays with the Top Spirit team.

Speaker note: Emphasise "no live connectors by default" and "ERP-agnostic".

Slide 4 — Three pillars

  1. Current- and target-state modelling — maps from exports, reports and interviews.
  2. AI agents inside Approval Envelopes — AI proposes; humans decide.
  3. Source-linked Audit Trail — every figure traces back to its source.

Visual: assets/mockups/01-solution-architecture.mmd; rendered fallback: assets/mockups/01-solution-architecture.png — see also the full process landscape in assets/mockups/03-process-landscape.mmd

Slide 5 — How it works: order-to-cash

As-is: PDF/email orders re-entered manually; price and stock checked separately.

To-be: AI extracts and validates the order; Customer Service approves the draft.

Visuals: assets/mockups/04-as-is-order-to-cash.mmd + assets/mockups/05-to-be-order-to-cash.mmd

Slide 6 — Human-in-the-loop governance

Risk-based tiers:

  • Tier 1 — act and log (low risk).
  • Tier 2 — AI proposes, human approves (operational).
  • Tier 3 — AI assists only, mandatory human decision (financial/pricing/customer-facing).

Visuals: assets/mockups/10-governance-tiers.mmd + assets/mockups/07-agent-approval-envelope.mmd; live-demo fallback: assets/mockups/walkthrough/index.html screen 4 (Governance Tiers).

Slide 7 — Trust: the Source-linked Audit Trail

Every action leaves a trace:

  • Trigger export or file.
  • AI action and confidence.
  • Evidence shown to the approver.
  • Approver decision and timestamp.
  • Final outcome and reference.

Visual: assets/mockups/11-source-linked-audit-trail.mmd

Slide 8 — Phasing and first value

Phase Outcome
Phase 0 — Foundations Rules, export inventory, AI-literacy pilot
Wave 1 — First value Mapped process, consolidated report, Power BI pilot, governance model
Wave 2+ — Scale Order-intake automation, reconciliation assistant, Business Glossary, ERP recommendation

Speaker note: Wave 1 is designed to prove the model in 4–6 weeks.

Slide 9 — What success looks like

  • Hours saved per automated task, logged.
  • Rebuilt reports and tools adopted by the team.
  • Processes and definitions captured and agreed.
  • A vendor-neutral ERP recommendation.
  • Team capability rises on a maturity scale.
