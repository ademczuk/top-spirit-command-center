How we prioritise

Each opportunity is tagged with Effort, Value and Risk. We sequence live roll-outs by looking for low-effort / high-value / low-risk candidates first, then move into higher-effort or higher-risk pilots only after the approval envelope and audit trail are proven. Steering-committee items stay as decision-support packs rather than automation until the Next-ERP path is clear.
