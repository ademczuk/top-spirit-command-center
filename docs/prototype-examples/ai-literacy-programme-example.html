AI literacy programme

Capability-building programme that leaves the client team able to own, maintain and extend AI-assisted workflows without depending on Angel Software Solutions long term.

Modules

1. Foundations

What AI can and cannot do; data classification; safe use of exports; project vocabulary.

2. Prompting & agents

Writing precise prompts; chaining tasks; validating outputs; spotting hallucinations.

3. Approval envelopes

Designing human-in-the-loop decisions; evidence rules; rejection and exception handling.

4. Source-linked audit

Lineage hashes, export IDs, transformation logs; tracing every figure back to source.

5. Power BI literacy

Importing classified exports, Power Query transforms, simple DAX, publishing guardrails.

6. ERP readiness

Mapping exports to a future ERP; migration risk; preserving agents through change.

Maturity levels

Aware

Understands the vocabulary, risks and approval rules. Can explain why a figure needs a source.

Practitioner

Can run prompts, review AI outputs and operate an approval envelope with supervision.

Owner

Can design a new envelope, maintain the glossary and reproduce a source-linked report.

Champion

Can coach others, lead a pilot expansion and advise the steering committee on AI risk.

Co-delivery note

Angel Software Solutions designs the programme, trains the first cohort and coaches the BICC lead to become the internal champion. Capability — prompts, glossaries, approval envelopes and audit standards — is documented and handed over so the client team can run future waves without external dependency. This is not a managed service; it is a capability transfer.
