Handover evidence checklist

Backlog: TASK-45 - Evidence of team maintaining tools after AI-literacy handover Status: Prepared checklist. Completion requires live client action and evidence.

What counts as evidence

The handover is complete only when a named Top Spirit / Marussia owner can operate the artifact without Angel driving the keyboard. The evidence should be attached to the controlled project tracker or meeting notes and referenced back into Backlog.

Evidence area Client owner to name Minimum evidence Pass condition
