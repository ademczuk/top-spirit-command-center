Engagement at a glance - Top Spirit (Marussia)

Internal working preview. Source material: project expectations brief. Scope, timeline and commercials to be agreed in the introductory call.

In one line

A capability-building process-review + automation + AI-literacy programme that creates value now - while the ERP migration and future BI-platform choice are both still undecided - built entirely on file exports, kept inside Microsoft 365 E3, with a human in control of every financial and customer-facing decision.

The three Angel Software Solutions pillars

The brief maps the engagement to three signature ideas that run through every deliverable:

  1. Current- and target-state modelling - process maps drawn from exports, reports and interviews.
  2. AI agents inside explicit Approval Envelopes - AI proposes; a human decides, especially for financial, pricing and customer-facing outputs.
  3. Source-linked Audit Trail - every figure traces back to the export it came from.

How we work - six non-negotiables

  • Export-layer first - file exports (Excel, CSV, JSON), not live connectors. ERP-agnostic and survives the migration.
  • Within budget - Microsoft 365 E3 only; no new licences, software or connectors without an evidence-backed business case.
  • Human oversight - financial, pricing and customer-facing outputs stay human-validated, never AI-decided.
  • Privacy - GDPR and data classification respected; no one sees data they are not authorised to see.
  • Knowledge transfer - co-delivered alongside the team, not a black box; artifacts and capability stay with them.
  • Iterative - wave-based; start small, prove value, then scale.
