Phase 0 - AI literacy pilot: Foundations

Goal: make the team safe and useful with LLMs before any agent touches financial, pricing or customer-facing work.

Audience

  • BICC / BI counterpart.
  • Finance, sales/commercial, customer service/order management, logistics and procurement SMEs.
  • AI Steering Committee sponsor or delegate.

Module 1: What LLMs are good and bad at

Outcomes - Explain that LLMs generate probabilistic text and need verification. - Recognise hallucination, stale knowledge and over-confident answers. - Use LLMs for drafting, summarising, extraction assistance and QA checklists, not unsupervised decisions.

Exercise - Give the model a neutral sales-report narrative and ask for assumptions, missing sources and validation questions.

Module 2: Data classification in practice

Outcomes - Apply T0/T1/T2/T3 classification to common exports and reports. - Decide what can be pasted into which tool. - Redact or aggregate before using AI where possible.

Exercise - Classify five sample snippets: public portfolio text, anonymised process notes, customer sales table, price list, bank export.

Module 3: Prompting with source discipline

Outcomes - Ask for source-linked outputs and uncertainty markers. - Require the model to separate facts, assumptions and recommendations. - Avoid pasting uncontrolled personal or confidential data.

Exercise prompt skeleton

You are helping document a process from authorised internal notes.
Return:
1. facts directly supported by the notes,
2. assumptions that need SME validation,
3. open questions,
4. draft process steps with source IDs.
Do not invent missing data.

Module 4: Approval Envelopes

Outcomes - Understand that AI proposes and humans decide. - Identify actions that require mandatory approval: pricing, financial postings, customer-facing emails, master-data changes. - Read an Approval Envelope log.

Exercise - Sort ten example AI outputs into: may auto-log, needs operational approval, or financial/customer-facing mandatory approval.

Pilot session format

Segment Duration Activity Artifact
Intro and guardrails 10 min Explain T0-T3 and human-in-loop rule Data-classification handout
LLM capability demo 20 min Prompting exercise with safe notes Prompt workbook
Approval Envelope lab 25 min Classify actions and design approvals Approval worksheet
Role-specific breakouts 30 min Finance/BICC/Sales examples Use-case notes
Debrief 15 min Feedback and Wave 1 training needs Feedback log

Feedback capture

  • Confidence before/after: 1-5.
  • "I can safely classify data before using AI": yes/no/comment.
  • "I can explain when AI may propose but not decide": yes/no/comment.
  • Top three workflows where AI could help.
  • Top three concerns/blockers.
