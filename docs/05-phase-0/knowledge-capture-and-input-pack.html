Phase 0 - Knowledge-capture and stakeholder input pack

Purpose: turn undocumented logic, tacit process knowledge and file-export reality into source-linked artifacts the team can maintain.

Session sequence

  1. Kickoff / scope confirmation: confirm Phase 0 + Wave 1 pilot boundaries and non-negotiables.
  2. Export inventory workshop: enumerate every candidate export/report and owner.
  3. Order-to-cash walkthrough: current-state process, pain points, manual/duplicated work.
  4. Manual report teardown: rebuild candidate report, source files, formulas, users.
  5. Glossary session: agree core definitions and unresolved conflicts.
  6. Governance session: approval envelopes for finance/pricing/customer-facing decisions.
  7. Opportunity triage: score automation candidates by effort/value/risk.

Interview guide

For each process/report/use case:

  • What triggers it?
  • Which system/export/report proves the trigger occurred?
  • Who owns the work and who reviews it?
  • What manual steps, re-keying, spreadsheets or copy/paste happen?
  • What errors or delays occur and how often?
  • What decisions are financial, pricing or customer-facing?
  • What data is confidential or GDPR-sensitive?
  • Which definition/formula is undocumented or disputed?
  • What would make the team trust an automated or AI-assisted output?
