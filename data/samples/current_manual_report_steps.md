# Current manual report build steps  -  sample

1. Export invoice lines from ERP.
2. Export stock/depletions report from warehouse or ERP.
3. Copy both into the monthly workbook.
4. Manually remove credit-note rows or adjust signs.
5. Calculate net sales, GP and depletions by channel/category.
6. Paste summary into management deck.
7. Finance/BICC checks totals against ERP report.

Source IDs: `EXP-ERP-SALES-001`, `EXP-ERP-STOCK-001`, `RPT-BICC-SALES-001`.
